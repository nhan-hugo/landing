Our idea :

Bringing France back to the plate! The objective is to recall the good old days, the red tablecloth, the glass of wine and the piece of meat. We must revisit our land and its local producers.

How to choose?

The card will remind you of your grandmother's recipes. It will remind you of Sunday meals, Christmas dinners or family celebrations. What do you remember most about your youth? Your instinct will guide you on the menu and on your plate.
